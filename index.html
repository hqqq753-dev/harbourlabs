About

Small studio, focused work.

Harbour Labs is an independent software studio. We build a small number of apps and stand behind each one. We don't take advertising, we don't sell user data, and we don't ship things we wouldn't use ourselves.

The studio is registered as Harbour Labs LLC in Wyoming, United States, and operates as a single-product company while our first app finds its audience.
