Dinnrd

iOS · 2026 · $4.99/month with a 7-day trial

A meal-planning app for households. Dinnrd plans the week's dinners around what's already in your fridge, flags food before it spoils, and saves recipes from anywhere on the internet.

What it does

  • Builds a weekly meal plan from what's actually in your fridge, not just what's in a recipe app
  • Tracks expiry dates and quietly nudges you to use food before it goes off
  • Saves recipes from any URL, photo, or video — TikTok, Instagram, blog posts, your camera roll
  • Designed for households of more than one, with shared planning and a shared shopping list
