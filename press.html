Company descriptions

Short (one sentence)

Standard (one paragraph)

Harbour Labs is an independent software studio that designs and ships consumer iOS apps. Its first product is Dinnrd, a meal-planning app for households. The studio works on one product at a time and is funded by app subscriptions. Harbour Labs was founded in 2026 by Hamish, a software developer, and is registered as Harbour Labs LLC in Wyoming, United States.
