About

A small studio building one thing at a time.

Harbour Labs is a Wyoming LLC that designs and ships consumer iOS apps. Its first product is Dinnrd, a meal-planning app for households.

What we do

We make consumer apps for iPhone. The focus is on tools that take a small but annoying part of daily life — meal planning, household admin, the kind of thing that takes too many decisions per day — and turn it into five minutes of work instead of fifty.

Every app is paid and supported indefinitely.

How we work

One product at a time. When an app is mature enough to maintain itself, the studio begins the next one. Running three things half-well doesn't appeal to us.

The business runs on subscription revenue, which pays for the people writing the code and the servers running it. That's the entire model.

Who's behind this

Harbour Labs is run by Hamish, a software developer. He started the studio in 2026 to publish Dinnrd under a proper company structure instead of as a personal project. The plan is to keep adding products under the Harbour Labs name as new ones are ready.

What's next

Dinnrd is the focus through 2026. A second app will start once Dinnrd is on its feet — probably another tool for the kitchen, though the specifics are still being figured out.
